Recipe #1

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: Up to 12 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti One Packet
Marinara Sauce 1 Cup
Water 3 Cups

Step 1: Pour 3 cups of water into a bowl.

Step 2: Place spaghetti into bowl and boil for 12 minutes.

Step 3: Add marinara sauce and mix for 3 minutes.

Step 4: Enjoy!!!

Recipe #2

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Bread Two pieces
Peanut Butter 1 teaspoon
Jelly 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Spread peanut butter onto one of the two bread pieces.

Step 2: Spread jelly onto the second bread piece.

Step 3: Put both pieces together.

Step 4: Enjoy!!!
